Cancellation/Refund Policies

At Talentocraft, we strive to provide the best experience for our users. We understand that sometimes, circumstances may require you to cancel or seek a refund for a purchase. Below are our policies regarding cancellations and refunds:

Cancellation Policy

If you wish to cancel a purchase made through Talentocraft, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Cancellations are subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of purchase may be eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made after 24 hours of purchase may not be eligible for a refund, depending on the circumstances.
  • Subscription cancellations may be subject to additional terms and conditions outlined in the subscription agreement.

Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase from Talentocraft. If you are not completely satisfied, please contact us to discuss your concerns. Refunds are subject to the following conditions:

  • Refunds may be issued at the discretion of Talentocraft's management.
  • Refunds will be processed using the same method of payment used for the original purchase.
  • Refunds may take up to [X] business days to reflect in your account.
  • Refunds for digital products may be subject to additional terms and conditions outlined in the product agreement.

Please note that Talentocraft reserves the right to update or modify these policies at any time without prior notice. It is your responsibility to review these policies periodically for changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about our cancellation or refund policies, please contact our customer support team for assistance.
