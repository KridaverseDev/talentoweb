Getting Started

How do I create an account on Talentocraft?

To create an account on Talentocraft, simply click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the website. Fill in the required information and follow the prompts to complete the registration process.

How do I apply for a job on Talentocraft?

To apply for a job on Talentocraft, browse through the available listings and click on the job you're interested in. Follow the instructions provided by the employer to submit your application.

How do I contact support for assistance?

If you need assistance or have any questions, you can contact our support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" link located at the bottom of the page. Fill out the form with your inquiry, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

How can I subscribe to receive updates and notifications?

To subscribe to updates and notifications from Talentocraft, simply go to your account settings and enable the option to receive notifications. You'll then start receiving relevant updates about job listings, new features, and more.

Account

How do I create an account on Talentocraft?

To create an account on Talentocraft, simply click on the "Sign Up" button located on the top-right corner of the homepage. Fill in the required information such as your name, email address, and password, then click "Sign Up" to complete the registration process.

How can I update my account information?

You can update your account information by logging into your Talentocraft account and navigating to the "Account Settings" section. From there, you can edit your profile details, change your password, update your email address, and manage other account settings as needed.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your email address associated with your Talentocraft account, and you will receive instructions on how to reset your password via email.

How can I delete my Talentocraft account?

To delete your Talentocraft account, please contact our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page. They will assist you in the account deletion process and ensure that your account information is permanently removed from our system.

Can I use my Talentocraft account on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use your Talentocraft account on multiple devices. Simply log in with your credentials on any device with internet access, and you'll have access to your account and all its features.

Pricing & License

Where can I see the licenses that I bought?

You can view the licenses you've purchased by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Licenses" or "License Management" section. There, you'll find a list of all the licenses you've bought along with their details.

Am I unable to continue using if my license expires?

If your license expires, you may lose access to certain features or updates depending on the terms of the license agreement. However, you can usually renew your license to continue using the product without interruption.

How many websites can I tie to one license?

The number of websites you can tie to one license depends on the terms of the license agreement. Some licenses may allow you to use the product on a single website, while others may permit use on multiple sites. Check your license agreement for specific details.

Do you have volume purchase programs?

Yes, we offer volume purchase programs for organizations or individuals who wish to purchase multiple licenses in bulk. Please contact our sales team for more information on volume pricing and discounts.

Are there any educational pricing or discounts?

Yes, we offer educational pricing and discounts for students, teachers, and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with proof of eligibility to avail of educational discounts on our products.
